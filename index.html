About Company

Our Vision

To empower global livestock productivity through advanced trace mineral nutrition.

Our Mission

To innovate, manufacture, and deliver bioavailable trace mineral solutions that are species-specific, residue-free, and globally compliant.

Why Choose Us

  • 100% Chelated / Organic Minerals
    Ensures superior absorption and bioavailability for optimal animal health.
  • Made in India – Export Ready
    Manufactured with global quality standards, ready for international markets.
  • ISO, FSSAI, GMP+ Certified
    Compliant with top-tier regulatory standards, ensuring safety and quality.
  • Veterinary Nutrition Science
    Developed with scientific precision to support animal wellness and performance.
